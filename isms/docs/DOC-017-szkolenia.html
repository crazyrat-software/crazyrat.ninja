⚠ Critical – Board MUST attend training (NIS2 requirement)

In accordance with Art. 21(1) NIS2, the management body must attend cybersecurity training to acquire the knowledge and skills sufficient to identify risks and evaluate cybersecurity risk management practices.

3. Training Content

Induction and recurring training (minimum):

  • Information Security Policy – basic principles (DOC-001),
  • Recognising phishing, smishing and vishing,
  • Secure passwords and use of MFA,
  • Information classification and data handling (DOC-010),
  • Incident reporting – how and when (DOC-005/006),
  • Secure remote work and BYOD,
  • Social engineering – recognising manipulation,
  • Clean desk / screen policy.

Board training (minimum):

  • Cybersecurity threat landscape in the sector,
  • NIS2 requirements and Board accountability,
  • Cybersecurity risk management – concepts,
  • Sector incident case studies,
  • Decision-making exercise (tabletop).
