1. Register Management Principles

  • Each asset is assigned to an owner responsible for its security and classification.
  • Register updated on every change: addition, removal or significant modification of an asset.
  • Full register review at least once every ▸ [12 months]min. 1×/year.
  • Register serves as input to the risk assessment process (DOC-002/003) and continuity planning (DOC-011).
